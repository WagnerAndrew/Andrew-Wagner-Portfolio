I’m a Full Stack Web Developer with previous work experience as an audio visual systems programmer and film production specialist.

Prior to graduating from Denver University’s Full Stack Web Development program, I worked for Denver’s largest AV integrator as a system programmer for globally recognized clients.

I programmed audio visual equipment systems and designed aesthetically pleasing user interfaces that were efficient and easy to use. Major projects included British Petroleum’s new North American headquarters, Colorado Rockies video playback systems, and University of Colorado’s Case Theater.

Before my tenure at Linx, I spent many years in Chicago as a film and television grip and lighting-technician with the IATSE Union Studio Mechanics Local 476. I worked on major motion pictures, network television shows and nationally licensed commercials.

Web development is an exciting field for me and I am looking forward to working with new clients and collaborators to make great applications.
